PORCHE 911 GT3 RS Boxer

The Porsche 911 GT RS3 also features a boxer six-cylinder engine, which is a characteristic feature of this model. Thanks to its low center of gravity and compact design, the boxer engine ensures excellent handling and dynamics on the road.

Producing over 700 horsepower, the engine delivers dynamic acceleration and high-speed capabilities. This enables the vehicle to achieve speeds of over 320 km/h in mere seconds.

An optimal torque ratio guarantees high responsiveness and efficiency on the road.

Equipped with a specialized fuel delivery system, the engine ensures peak performance and fuel efficiency, making the Porsche 911 GT3 RS with a boxer engine the preferred choice for enthusiasts seeking both performance and economy.

Leveraging advanced fuel management technologies, the boxer engine of the Porsche 911 GT3 RS achieves optimal efficiency while maintaining minimal emissions of harmful substances.
